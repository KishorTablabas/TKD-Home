CTMS

CTMS stands for Comprehensive Transport Management System. The system is built to simplify the procedures applied to logistic services. It is basically a software panel whose objective is to save time and enhance various sections of the transportation and logistics operations. CTMS encloses processes namely route planning, fleet management, order tracking, load optimization etc. CTMS software is repeatedly used by transportation companies, logistics providers and supply chain managers. Using this software is beneficial for one and all. It improves productivity, cost-efficiency and provides a visual idea of their transportation processes. Using CTMS software simplifies the effort and makes the user aware regarding the whereabouts of their valuables in conduct.
